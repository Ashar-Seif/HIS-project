Our Service

Dialysis & Kidney Care Center highly regards the health and safety of our patients and team members. We are closely monitoring the coronavirus (COVID-19) situation and all new developments. Our organization is actively reinforcing our extensive infection control practices to ensure the protection of all those within our facilities.

24 Hour Support

Our organization provides 24-hour service with experienced emergency medical specialists and dynamic staff.

Emergency Services

In an emergency, it is vital that you receive treatment as soon as possible.
If you suddenly become seriously ill for any reason, you should immediately dial 999 for an ambulance.

Medical Counseling

Our counselling services are provided by certified counsellors who deliver confidential personal counselling.

Driving innovation
